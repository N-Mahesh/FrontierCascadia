What this is

A build day for people who are done working in a vacuum.

We have watched talented students ship impressive demos on their own, then stall because no one was there to challenge the assumptions. This event exists to change that.

You will define a problem, cut it to what can actually ship, and build alongside mentors who have made the tradeoffs you are about to face.

FAQ

Answers we can stand behind.

Click a question to open the answer. If you have a unique situation, reach out.

Do I need a team already?
No. If you come alone, we will help you meet people quickly and build by lunch. We keep a team board and a matching table.
What if I am new to coding?
You are welcome. We provide starter templates, mentors, and scoped tracks so you can ship a real demo without deep experience.
Is there a fee and is aid available?
There is a small fee to cover food and space, and we offer waivers. You can request aid during registration with no extra steps.
What counts as a completed project?
A real demo with a clear user flow, a working core feature, and a short plan for what you would build next.
Can I use AI tools?
Yes, as long as you can explain what you shipped and how it works. We value understanding and ownership.
How do I get updates?
Register and you will receive the prep packet and schedule updates. For urgent questions, email the team directly.
